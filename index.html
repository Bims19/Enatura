About Enatura LLC

Sustainable Utility Solutions Built for the Future

Enatura LLC is a utility services company providing environmentally sound solutions, including equipment sales and services for power generation, electricity consumption, potable water, wastewater, and HVAC systems.

We focus on environmentally justified and economically viable results for public utilities, engineering firms, hotels, hospitals, schools, and industrial sites.

From steam generation and grey water systems to renewable energy and environmental consulting, we help implement planet-friendly infrastructure across Nigeria and beyond.

Our services include in-house system design, installation, operations & maintenance, and water quality testing for private clients and government bodies.

🌍 Our Mission

To deliver innovative, eco-conscious utility solutions that empower communities, industries, and governments to achieve sustainable development goals.

🚀 Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading provider of sustainable infrastructure, inspiring cleaner, smarter, and greener environments for all.

Power Generation

Enatura provides reliable, cost-effective power solutions for industries facing rising electricity demands. Their field services cover maintenance, troubleshooting, condition monitoring, and generator testing, ensuring optimal efficiency for hotels, hospitals, schools, and industrial sites.

Electricity Consumption Optimization

Enatura helps businesses reduce energy consumption, lower costs, and improve efficiency with tailored solutions for inductive motor power control. Their cost-effective products guarantee energy savings, utility demand reduction, and decreased carbon emissions.

Environmental Services

Enatura partners with global experts to deliver compliance consultancy, certified personnel, and sustainability services. Their capabilities include environmental studies on freshwater, wetlands, marine ecosystems, wildlife, endangered species, and transportation. They ensure consistent, professional solutions for managing and operating facilities, whether compliant or non-compliant, while addressing health, safety, risk, and social consulting needs.
